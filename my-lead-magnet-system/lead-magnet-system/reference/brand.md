industries — who are **already making money and have clients** but keep
**hitting the same income ceiling**.

Write TO business owners who:
- love what they do
- have clients
- are making money
- are serious about growing
- are tired of hitting the same income number
- want to move the income ceiling
- need help finding the gaps inside the business

Do NOT write to **beginners, hobbyists, or people in survival mode.**
